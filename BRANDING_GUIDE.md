## Usage Guidelines

### When to Use "AikidoDigital"

✅ **Always use "AikidoDigital" in:**
- Company name references
- Product name
- Footer branding
- Page titles
- About page
- Email signatures
- All marketing copy

**Examples:**
- "AikidoDigital provides simple membership management..."
- "Contact AikidoDigital at info@aikidodigital.com"
- "About AikidoDigital"
- Footer: "© 2026 Aikido Digital Limited. All rights reserved."

### When to Use "aikidodigital.com"

✅ **Use aikidodigital.com when:**
- Referring to the marketing website URL
- In technical documentation
- In README files
- In deployment configurations

### When to Use "aikido.digital"

✅ **Use aikido.digital when:**
- Referring to the members-only product portal
- Technical documentation about the product
- Developer documentation

❌ **DO NOT use aikido.digital:**
- In marketing copy as the brand name
- As the contact email domain
- In the footer or about sections of the marketing site

## Typography & Presentation

### Correct Usage

✅ **AikidoDigital** - Product/company name  
✅ **aikidodigital.com** - Marketing website URL  
✅ **aikido.digital** - Product portal URL (members only)  
✅ **info@aikidodigital.com** - Contact email  
✅ **@aikidodigital** - Twitter handle  

### Incorrect Usage

❌ **aikido.digital** - As brand name  
❌ **Aikido Digital** - With space (except in legal "Aikido Digital Limited")  
❌ **info@aikido.digital** - Wrong email domain  
❌ **aikido digital** - Lowercase with space

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ Using "aikido.digital" as the brand name in marketing materials
2. ❌ Using info@aikido.digital email address
3. ❌ Lowercase "aikidodigital" when referring to the company
4. ❌ Adding spaces "Aikido Digital" in product references
5. ❌ Confusing the two websites (aikidodigital.com vs aikido.digital)
