## Rationale

### Why a Simplified Privacy Policy

This marketing website (aikidodigital.com) has a minimal privacy policy because:

1. **No Data Collection on Marketing Site**
   - No cookies
   - No analytics tracking
   - No forms
   - No visitor data storage

2. **Email Only Contact**
   - Only data collected: emails sent directly to info@aikidodigital.com
   - Handled through standard email practices
   - No automated collection or storage

3. **GDPR Handled in Contracts**
   - Platform GDPR compliance is in individual organisation contracts
   - Member data belongs to the organisations, not AikidoDigital
   - Contracts specify data processing agreements

4. **Clear Separation**
   - Marketing site (aikidodigital.com) ≠ Platform (aikido.digital)
   - Different privacy requirements for each
   - Policy clearly states this distinction

**A:** Not extensively. The marketing site doesn't process personal data (except email). Platform GDPR compliance is in contracts.

### Q: What if someone asks to exercise GDPR rights?
**A:** 
- Marketing site visitor: "We don't collect any data from the website."

### Q: Should we add a cookie banner?
